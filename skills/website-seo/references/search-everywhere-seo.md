# Search Everywhere SEO

Multi-platform search optimization. Search-like discovery increasingly happens across multiple platforms — social media, AI assistants, marketplaces, forums, and video — not just Google.

---

## The Multi-Platform Search Landscape

Search is no longer synonymous with Google. Users discover content across multiple platforms, each with its own algorithm and ranking factors.

### Search Behavior by Platform (2025-2026)

| Platform | Monthly Searches / Users | Primary Demographics | Search Type |
|----------|------------------------|---------------------|-------------|
| Google | ~8.5B searches/day | All demographics | Comprehensive |
| YouTube | ~3B searches/day | All, skewing 18-49 | Video, how-to, reviews |
| TikTok | ~3B+ monthly searches | Gen Z, Millennials | Discovery, trends, reviews |
| Reddit | ~1.7B monthly visits | 18-49, tech-savvy | Community answers, reviews |
| Pinterest | ~500M monthly users | Women 25-54, lifestyle | Visual discovery, shopping |
| LinkedIn | ~1B members | Professionals, B2B | Career, industry, B2B |
| Amazon | ~300M+ active users | Shoppers | Product search |

### Why Multi-Platform Matters

- ~40% of Gen Z prefer TikTok or Instagram over Google for discovery searches
- Reddit results in Google SERPs grew ~1,328% between 2023-2024
- YouTube is the second largest search engine globally
- Google increasingly surfaces social/platform content in SERPs (Reddit, TikTok, YouTube)
- Platform-specific SEO creates multiple discovery channels beyond Google dependence

## TikTok SEO

### TikTok Search Ranking Factors

| Factor | Weight | Optimization |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Video caption/text | High | Include target keywords naturally in caption |
| On-screen text | High | Add keyword-rich text overlays |
| Spoken words | High | Say your keywords — TikTok transcribes audio |
| Hashtags | Moderate | Mix broad (#cooking) and specific (#30minutemeals) |
| Engagement | High | Comments, shares, saves, watch time |
| Account authority | Moderate | Niche consistency, follower engagement rate |
| Sound/music | Low-Moderate | Trending sounds boost discovery (not search) |

### TikTok Content Optimization

**Caption optimization:**
- Front-load keywords in the first line (visible without "more" tap)
- 150-300 characters for search-targeted content
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags (mix of broad and niche)
- Use question format when targeting "how to" queries

**Video optimization for search:**
- Add text overlays with target keywords in the first 3 seconds
- Say the keyword aloud in the first 5 seconds
- Hook structure: problem → solution → detail → CTA
- Optimal length for search: 60-180 seconds (longer than viral content)
- Use closed captions (auto-generated or manual)

**Content types that rank on TikTok search:**
- How-to tutorials and step-by-step guides
- Product reviews and comparisons
- "Day in the life" / behind-the-scenes
- List content ("5 tools for...", "3 ways to...")
- Before/after transformations

### TikTok → Website Traffic

- Link in bio (use link-in-bio tool for multiple URLs)
- Mention your website/brand name verbally and in text overlay
- Create TikTok content that surfaces in Google SERPs (Google indexes public TikTok videos)
- Use TikTok Analytics to track profile views and website clicks

## Reddit SEO

### Reddit's Visibility in Google

Reddit has become a dominant presence in Google search results. Google's "Perspectives" and "Discussions and forums" features prominently surface Reddit content.

**Why this matters:**
- Google has a content licensing deal with Reddit (announced 2024)
- Reddit results appear in SERPs for product reviews, recommendations, and "best of" queries
- Users append "reddit" to searches for authentic opinions (~300M+ searches/month)

### Optimizing for Reddit's Presence in Google

**What gets surfaced:**
- Top-voted answers in relevant subreddits
- Threads with genuine community discussion
- Recent, active threads (freshness matters)
- r/AskReddit, r/personalfinance, r/technology — high-authority subreddits

**Strategy (not marketing — community participation):**
- Genuinely participate in subreddits relevant to your industry
- Provide helpful, detailed answers (not promotional)
- Build karma and reputation through consistent, valuable contributions
- Share expertise that demonstrates first-hand experience
- Create AMA (Ask Me Anything) threads if you have genuine authority

**What NOT to do:**
- Post promotional content (will be removed, possibly banned)
- Use alt accounts for upvoting (Reddit detects and bans this)
- Drop links to your site without context
- Post generic marketing content

### Reddit's Internal Search

- Reddit's search is keyword-based — title and post body matter
- Post titles should be descriptive and include relevant keywords
- Comment content is searchable within Reddit
- Subreddit relevance is the strongest filter

## YouTube Search

### YouTube vs Google Search

| Aspect | Google | YouTube |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Content type | All formats | Video only |
| Ranking signals | Relevance, authority, experience | Watch time, engagement, CTR |
| User intent | Information, transactions, navigation | Learn, watch, be entertained |
| Freshness | Moderate importance | High importance for trending topics |
| Click-through | SERP CTR | Thumbnail + title CTR |

→ See `video-seo.md` for comprehensive YouTube optimization, VideoObject schema, and video sitemaps.

### YouTube → Google SERP Integration

YouTube videos appear in Google results via:
- Video carousels (horizontal scrolling video results)
- Featured snippets (video answers)
- "Videos" tab in Google Search
- AI Overviews with video citations
- Standard organic results (YouTube URLs)

**Optimization for Google SERP video features:**
- Use VideoObject schema on pages embedding YouTube videos
- Include Key Moments / chapters in video description
- Create descriptive, keyword-rich video titles
- Write detailed video descriptions (first 2-3 lines most important)

## Pinterest SEO

### Pinterest as a Visual Search Engine

Pinterest functions more like a search engine than a social network. Users actively search for ideas, products, and inspiration.

### Pin Optimization

| Element | Optimization |
|---------|-------------|
| Pin title | Include primary keyword, 40-100 characters |
| Pin description | Keyword-rich, 150-300 characters, natural language |
| Image | Vertical 2:3 ratio (1000×1500px), high quality, text overlay |
| Alt text | Descriptive, keyword-aware (same rules as web images) |
| URL | Link to relevant page on your website |
| Board name | Category keyword (e.g., "Healthy Dinner Recipes" not "My Food Board") |

### Pinterest SEO Strategy

- **Board optimization:** Name boards with keyword-rich titles, write keyword descriptions
- **Rich Pins:** Enable for products (price, availability), articles (headline, author), recipes (ingredients, cook time)
- **Seasonal planning:** Pin seasonal content 30-45 days before the season (Pinterest's algorithm surfaces seasonal content early)
- **Consistency:** Pin regularly (3-10 pins/day through scheduling tools — quality and relevance matter more than volume)
- **Fresh content:** New pins (new images) perform better than re-pins of old content

### Pinterest → Google

Pinterest pins are indexed by Google and can appear in Google Image Search. High-quality pins with proper alt text and descriptions gain dual visibility.

## LinkedIn Search

### LinkedIn Content Search Ranking

| Factor | Impact | Optimization |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Keyword relevance | High | Use industry keywords in posts and articles |
| Engagement (first 90 min) | High | Post when your network is active; encourage comments |
| Profile authority | Moderate | Complete profile, endorsements, recommendations |
| Content format | Moderate | Document posts and polls get more reach than plain text |
| Hashtags | Low-Moderate | 3-5 relevant hashtags per post |

### LinkedIn Profile Optimization for Search

- **Headline:** Use keywords your audience searches for (not just your job title)
- **About section:** Front-load keywords in the first 2 lines (visible without "see more")
- **Experience:** Keyword-rich descriptions of relevant work
- **Skills:** Add and get endorsed for keywords related to your expertise
- **Articles:** LinkedIn articles are indexed by Google — use for SEO content

### LinkedIn → Google

LinkedIn profiles and articles rank in Google search results:
- Personal profiles rank for name searches
- Company pages rank for brand searches
- LinkedIn articles can rank for industry keywords
- Ensure your LinkedIn content matches your website's SEO messaging

## App Store Optimization (ASO) — Brief Overview

If the business has a mobile app, basic ASO principles apply:

| Element | iOS (App Store) | Android (Play Store) |
|---------|----------------|---------------------|
| App name | 30 characters, front-load keywords | 30 characters, front-load keywords |
| Subtitle | 30 characters | N/A (use short description) |
| Keyword field | 100 characters (hidden, comma-separated) | N/A (extracted from description) |
| Description | Not indexed for search | Indexed for search — use keywords |
| Screenshots | 10 max, first 3 most visible | 8 max, first 3 most visible |
| Reviews/ratings | Major ranking factor | Major ranking factor |

## Cross-Platform Content Strategy

### Content Repurposing Framework

One piece of content → multiple platforms:

```
Blog Post (primary content)
  ├── YouTube video (full version)
  │   ├── TikTok clips (60-90 second segments)
  │   ├── YouTube Shorts (vertical clips)
  │   └── Instagram Reels (vertical clips)
  ├── LinkedIn article (professional angle)
  │   └── LinkedIn carousel (key takeaways)
  ├── Pinterest pins (visual stats, infographics)
  ├── Reddit comment (genuine expertise sharing)
  └── Twitter/X thread (key points)
```

### Platform-Specific Adaptation Rules

Don't just repost — adapt for each platform's format and audience:

| Platform | Adapt For | Avoid |
|----------|----------|-------|
| TikTok | Casual, personality-forward, visual storytelling | Corporate tone, long intros, hard sells |
| YouTube | Depth, tutorials, personality, thumbnails | Reading blog posts on camera, poor audio |
| Reddit | Genuine expertise, helpful answers, community norms | Self-promotion, marketing speak, link dropping |
| Pinterest | Visual appeal, vertical format, actionable ideas | Poor image quality, landscape orientation |
| LinkedIn | Professional insights, industry perspective, data | Casual memes, off-topic content, clickbait |

### Cross-Platform Attribution & Tracking

- Use UTM parameters for all platform → website links
- Track referral traffic from each platform in GA4
- Monitor brand search volume growth (indicates cross-platform brand building)
- Track platform-specific engagement metrics (followers, engagement rate, saves)
- Cross-reference platform growth with organic search performance
